# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users
Developers, recruiters, and hiring managers reviewing portfolio work to evaluate frontend craft, product thinking, and execution quality. They browse quickly, usually on desktop first, and decide within minutes whether to continue to deeper sections or reach out.

## Product Purpose
This portfolio exists to convert first impressions into interview and hiring opportunities for frontend and web roles. Success means visitors quickly understand strengths, trust implementation quality, and have a clear path to contact.

## Brand Personality
Bold, clean, fast. The tone is confident and direct, with strong visual clarity, minimal friction, and a high-craft feel that signals production readiness rather than template-driven presentation.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS template look (predictable hero + card grid + muted palette patterns).
- Safe, overused landing-page scaffolds that feel interchangeable with AI-generated demos.

## Design Principles
- Show capability through real interface quality and interaction polish, not broad claims.
- Prioritize clarity of value and proof so core strengths are legible at a glance.
- Keep momentum high by reducing cognitive load and interaction friction across the journey.
- Use distinct brand decisions that feel intentional and memorable, not category defaults.
- Make conversion obvious with clear, trustworthy pathways to contact and next action.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG 2.2 AA baseline across key surfaces. Maintain readable contrast, visible focus states, keyboard navigability, reduced-motion alternatives, and clear semantic structure so core content is accessible across assistive needs.
